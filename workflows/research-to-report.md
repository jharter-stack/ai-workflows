# Research to Report Workflow

## Goal
Turn raw source material into a clean summary with key findings, risks, and action items.

## Best For
- research summaries
- internal reports
- client briefings
- decision support

## Inputs
- source material
- project goal
- audience
- report format
- desired depth

## Steps
1. Gather all source material
2. Summarize each source
3. Extract major patterns and repeated themes
4. Identify risks, gaps, or contradictions
5. Draft an executive summary
6. Rewrite for the target audience
7. Generate action items and next steps

## Prompts Used
- source-summary
- trend-scan
- executive-brief-builder
- action-item-generator

## Output
A structured report with summary, findings, risks, and recommended next actions.

## Notes
- Best when source quality is decent
- Strong for messy notes, articles, reports, and mixed research inputs
- Add formatting rules if you need a specific report layout
