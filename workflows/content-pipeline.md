# Content Pipeline Workflow

## Goal
Turn a rough idea or keyword into a finished content draft using a structured AI workflow.

## Best For
- blog content
- newsletters
- educational content
- brand publishing

## Inputs
- topic or keyword
- target audience
- business or brand
- content goal
- tone
- format

## Steps
1. Define the topic, audience, and objective
2. Build the content brief
3. Generate an outline
4. Expand each section into a draft
5. Rewrite for clarity and flow
6. Add calls to action
7. Final polish for tone and consistency

## Prompts Used
- seo-brief-builder
- article-outline
- draft-expander
- rewrite-for-clarity
- call-to-action-generator

## Output
A cleaner first draft that is faster to review, edit, and publish.

## Notes
- Useful for repeatable publishing
- Can be adapted for blogs, email, landing pages, and social threads
- Better results come from tight audience definition
