# Idea to Publish Workflow

## Goal
Take a raw content or product idea and move it into publish-ready form with defined steps.

## Best For
- creators
- solo operators
- small teams
- brand content systems

## Inputs
- raw idea
- audience
- channel
- goal
- tone
- constraints

## Steps
1. Clarify the raw idea
2. Identify the audience and desired outcome
3. Turn the idea into a structured concept
4. Generate format options
5. Draft the chosen format
6. Refine for clarity, tone, and usefulness
7. Create final publish-ready copy

## Prompts Used
- idea-expander
- audience-angle-finder
- format-selector
- draft-builder
- rewrite-for-clarity

## Output
A rough idea transformed into a usable draft or publish-ready asset.

## Notes
- Great for reducing stall-out at the blank page stage
- Helps turn scattered thoughts into a repeatable workflow
- Works well for articles, posts, scripts, and emails
